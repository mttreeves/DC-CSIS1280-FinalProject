About:

Genshin Impact is an Open-World, Action RPG which can be played in both single and multiplayer. Have up to 4 characters in your party and perform combos between different party members to deal immense damage to all your foes.

  • Inital Release Date: September 28, 2020
  • Producer(s)/Developer(s): Hoyoverse (Formerly Mihoyo)
  • Composer(s): Yu-Peng Chen
  • Game Genre: Open-World, Action, RPG
  • Platforms: Windows, Google Play, App Store, PS4, PS5
  • Awards: The Game Award for The Best Mobile Game

Storyline:

You're a Traveler from an unknown world. Alongside your twin, you succumbed to an unknown god's powers and somehow you've woken up in the land of Teyvat. Alone. Shortly after waking, you meet Paimon who now accompanies you in your mission to find your twin. Along the way, you'll meet and help many different civilizations. Where will this journey take you Traveller?
